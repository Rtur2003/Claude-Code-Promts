## How to Use These Examples

1. **Read the scenario** - Understand what problem is being solved
2. **See the prompt in action** - Observe how the prompt guides the process
3. **Follow the methodology** - Apply the same approach to your projects
4. **Adapt to your needs** - Customize based on your specific requirements
